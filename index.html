Individuals are continuously changing their preferences, styles, and personalities with advancing technology. Due to the ever-changing tech advancements, UX researchers need to understand consumer behaviors for organizations. User research is a vital aspect of UX and UI. Comprehending how users interact on an organization's website is a fundamental part of designing a functional website. An example of implementing users research into a UX project is examining the difference between users shopping on a desktop browser versus a mobile browser. User research pulls together the “why” behind the UX/UI design while keeping the consumer in mind.
